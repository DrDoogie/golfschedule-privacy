Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 20, 2026

Golf Round Planner & Diary respects your privacy.

This app is designed to help users manage golf schedules, round records, scores, partners, photos, statistics, maps, weather lookups and backups.

Information We Collect

This app does not require account registration.

The app may store the following information locally on your device:

* Golf round schedules
* Golf course names
* Partner names
* Scores
* Memos
* Round photos
* App settings
* Backup data created by the user

How Information Is Used

The information is used only to provide app features such as:

* Managing golf schedules
* Recording round history
* Tracking scores and statistics
* Managing partner information
* Saving round photos
* Creating and importing backup files
* Opening map, restaurant and weather search results

Local Storage

Most app data is stored locally on your device.

If you create a backup file, that file is created by the user and may be saved or shared using Apple’s file sharing features.

Third-Party Services

This app may open external services such as Apple Calendar, Naver Map, Google Maps or web search pages when the user chooses to use related features.

This app does not control the privacy practices of those external services.

Photos and Camera

The app may request access to your photo library or camera only when you choose to add or take round photos.

Photos selected or taken by the user are stored for round record purposes.

Calendar Access

The app may request access to Apple Calendar only when calendar synchronization is used.

Calendar access is used to create or update golf round schedules.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent or share your personal data with third parties.

Children’s Privacy

This app is not specifically directed to children under 13.
